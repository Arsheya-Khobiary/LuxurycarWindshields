## Step 7 — Featured snippet & zero-click strategy

### 10 snippet-targeted answers (use as H2 + short paragraph)

1. **What is windscreen repair?**  
   Windscreen repair usually means **injecting resin** into a **small chip** to **stop spreading** and **restore strength**. It suits **limited damage**; **large or critical-zone cracks** often need **replacement**.

2. **What is windscreen replacement?**  
   Replacement removes the **damaged laminated screen** and fits **new glass** with **correct adhesive and safety standards**, then **ADAS calibration** if required.

3. **How much does windscreen replacement cost in London?**  
   Cost varies by **vehicle**, **glass brand (OEM vs aftermarket)**, **sensors**, and **mobile call-out**. Expect **professional quotes** after **registration**; avoid fixed site-wide prices unless you honour them.

4. **How long does a mobile windscreen replacement take?**  
   Often **under 90 minutes** of **work time** after access and prep; **cure times** may require **waiting before driving** — your fitter should state **safe drive-away time**.

5. **Same-day windscreen replacement London — is it possible?**  
   **Yes, when** the **correct glass** is **in stock** and a **technician** is **free**. Early contact with **reg + postcode** improves chances.

6. **Is it safe to drive with a cracked windscreen?**  
   **Not always.** If the crack **impairs vision** or **structural integrity** is in doubt, **stop** and use **mobile service** or **recovery**.

7. **Does mobile windscreen replacement include disposal?**  
   Reputable mobile fitters **remove and dispose** of **old glass** per **waste rules**; confirm when booking.

8. **Emergency car glass — what counts?**  
   **Smash-and-grab**, **highway debris**, or **cracks in the line of sight** often need **urgent** service to **secure** the vehicle and **restore visibility**.

9. **Repair vs replacement — quick rule?**  
   **Small, fresh chip** → may be repairable. **Long crack, edge crack, driver view** → usually **replacement**.

10. **Why choose mobile windscreen service in London?**  
    Avoids **driving** an **unsafe** car through **congestion**; **saves time**; **fitting** at **home or work** within **coverage area**.
